Stories from the Village

Morning on the Farm: My day begins with fresh air, chirping birds, and warm chai. We feed the cows, sweep the yard, and cook using firewood—it’s a peaceful start that I cherish every day.

Traditional Cooking: Whether it's dosa on a clay stove or pickles under the sun, every recipe has love and tradition passed down by my grandmother.
